# Welcome to the PS3 Gentoo Wiki

Welcome to the **PS3 Gentoo Wiki**! This wiki is dedicated to providing comprehensive resources, guides, and troubleshooting information for installing and running **Gentoo Linux** on a **PlayStation 3** (PS3). Whether you're a beginner or an advanced user, you'll find valuable instructions to help you set up Gentoo, troubleshoot common issues, and optimize your PS3 for the best performance.

## What You'll Find Here:

- **Installation Guides**: Step-by-step instructions to install Gentoo Linux on your PS3.
